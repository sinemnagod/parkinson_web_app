About the disease

What is Parkinson's Disease?

Parkinson's disease is a progressive neurological disorder that affects movement and muscle control — including the muscles that control facial expressions.

Progressive Condition

Parkinson's develops slowly over years. Early detection gives patients and doctors more time to manage symptoms and slow progression effectively.

Facial Hypomimia

One of the earliest signs of Parkinson's is "facial masking" — reduced ability to make facial expressions, due to muscle rigidity caused by the disease.

AI-Based Screening

Our model was trained on 244 YouTube videos of Parkinson's patients and healthy individuals, learning subtle differences in facial expression patterns.

How it works

Four steps to your result

The entire process takes under 2 minutes and requires only your camera.

01

02

03

AI Analysis

04
